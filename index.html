The Analytical Journey

From identifying operational bottlenecks in Prayagraj to delivering a data-backed roadmap for 2025.

1

Problem Definition

Identified stockouts during "Lagan" (Wedding) seasons and capital tied up in dead stock.

2

Data Collection

Digitized manual records: 80 sales transactions and 22 supplier logs (2023-2025).

3

Analysis

Applied Time-Series Forecasting, Pareto Analysis, and Supplier Lag correlation.

4

Solution

Delivered a Seasonal Inventory Plan and Supplier Diversification Strategy.

Key Data Insights

The "Boom & Bust" Cycle

Profits spike 10x during Oct-Nov (Festive) vs. Monsoon lows.

Profit Concentration

"Bed" and "Custom" categories drive nearly 64% of total profit.

Supply Chain Bottleneck Detected

Analysis of supplier logs revealed that "Sheesham Crafts Inc." is the primary cause of production delays, averaging 5.5 days late per order.

  • Bharat Hardware: 0.33 Days (Reliable)
  • Prayag Timbers: 1.17 Days (Reliable)
  • Sheesham Crafts Inc.: 5.50 Days (Critical Risk)

Strategic Recommendations

Seasonal Inventory Plan

Proactively increase stock of top 10 "Star Products" by 30% before September to capture the forecasted ₹1.67 Lakh peak profit in 2025.

"Bundle & Clear" Strategy

Liquidate dead stock (e.g., Rocking Chairs) by bundling them with high-demand beds. Reinvest recovered capital into high-margin Custom orders.

Supplier Diversification

Reduce risk by shifting 50% of orders from "Sheesham Crafts Inc." to alternative local suppliers in Prayagraj (e.g., Rajesh Timber Store).
