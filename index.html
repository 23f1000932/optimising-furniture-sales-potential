Critical Finding

Supply Chain Bottleneck Detected

Analysis of 22 supplier logs revealed that while most partners deliver within 24 hours, "Sheesham Crafts Inc." averages a 5.5 day delay, causing 80% of production halts.

Reliable Partner Bharat Hardware (0.3d)
Risk Factor Sheesham Crafts (5.5d)

Strategic Roadmap

Seasonal Inventory Plan

Use the 32.6% growth forecast to procure top 10 items in September, preventing the annual November stockout.

Target Impact: +₹1.67 Lakh Profit

"Bundle & Clear"

Liquidate dead stock (e.g., Rocking Chairs) by bundling with high-demand Beds. Reinvest capital into Custom orders.

Target Impact: Free ₹1L Capital

Supplier Diversification

Reduce dependency on "Sheesham Crafts" by qualifying 2 local backups (e.g., Rajesh Timber Store) for critical wood supplies.

Target Impact: < 2 Day Delay
